# Design System (High-Key Aesthetic)

This document defines the visual language for the 2mobilya premium furniture platform.

## Aesthetics
- **High-Key Design**: Bright, airy, and trustworthy.
- **Negative Space**: Generous whitespace to let products "breathe".
- **Soft Shapes**: Slightly rounded corners (border-radius 8px-16px) for cards and UI elements.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Saf Beyaz (#FFFFFF).
- **Secondary Background**: Sıcak Kum (#F9F8F6).
- **Accent (Primary)**: Koyu Antrasit (#1A1A1A) - for primary buttons and text.
- **Accent (Secondary)**: Adaçayı Yeşili (Sage Green - #7D8F7F) - for highlights and subtle accents.
- **Surface**: Off-white/Ghost white for subtle differentiation.

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Playfair Display` or `Cormorant` (Serif). Elegant, luxury feel.
- **Body & Forms**: `Inter` or `Figtree` (Sans-Serif). Clean, highly readable.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**: Sage Green or Anthracite with subtle hover effects (opacity shift or slight scale).
- **Cards**: Minimal border or soft shadow on hover. No harsh borders.
- **Input Fields**: Thin gray borders, Sage Green focus rings.

## Animations
- **Transitions**: Smooth, subtle Framer Motion transitions (duration 0.4s-0.6s).
- **Hover States**: Micro-interactions for engagement.
- **Modal overlays**: Blur background (backdrop-filter: blur(8px)).
